Profile

David was born in the Bay Area but grew up in a suburb of Chicago. After receiving his Bachelors of Science in Environmental Policy from the University of Michigan in 1999, he began his career at Northwestern Mutual in Ann Arbor, Michigan.

Since moving back to the Bay Area in 2006, David continues to build a comprehensive financial planning practice and has added a team of professionals that provide exceptional knowledge and service throughout the entire planning process for clients.

David is a member of several professional associations, such as the National Association of Insurance and Financial Advisors, MDRT and the National Association of Certified Financial Planners.
